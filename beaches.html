Beautiful Beaches Around The World

Copacabana, Brazil

Known for its 4 km stretch of golden beach life.

Famous food: Fried shrimp, açaí bowls

Size: 4 km

Santa Monica, USA

Known for its pier, surfing spots, and sunsets.

Famous food: Chowder, lobster bisque

Size: 21.8 km²

South Beach, Miami

Popular for nightlife and art deco vibes.

Famous food: Stubborn Seeds, crab

Size: 2.5 sq mi

Renaissance Island, Aruba

Private island with flamingo and iguana beaches.

Famous food: Sushi, Dutch pancakes

Size: 0.48 km²

Waikiki, Hawaii

Historic Hawaiian beach with great surf and sunsets.

Famous food: Shave ice, pie

Size: 2 miles

Goa, India

Known for nightlife, parties, and seafood.

Famous food: Bebinca, Ros omelette

Size: 3,700 km²

Kovalam, Kerala

Crescent-shaped beaches with calm waters.

Famous food: Coconut-based seafood

Size: 4.98 km²

Radhanagar Beach

One of Asia’s best beaches in the Andaman Islands.

Famous food: Bhel, chaat

Size: 2 km

Juhu Beach, Mumbai

Popular Mumbai beach facing the Arabian Sea.

Famous food: Pav bhaji

Size: 6 km
